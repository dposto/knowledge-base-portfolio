    How-To: Screen-Share

    This article provides procedures for initiating a screen-share session with a user's computer using Endpoint Central or BeyondTrust.
    Author David Posto
    Document Type Knowledge Base Article
    Audience Help Desk Agents
    Last Updated June 2026

    Symptoms

    How to initiate a screen-share session with a user's computer.

    Solution

    Step 1: Important Information

    1. ⚠️ NEVER take a screenshot of protected health information (PHI).

    2. If the device is a Mac, Apple, or mobile device:

      1. We are unable to screen-share into these devices.

      2. Note the following in the Service Order:

        1. The type of device.

        2. That the device cannot be screen-shared into.

      3. Return to the Originating Article and attempt to assist the caller as able.

    3. For non-dedicated agents:

      1. Screen-share accounts are provided to dedicated agents only.

      2. If you were prompted to initiate a screen-share, return to the Originating Article and attempt to assist the caller as able.

    4. For dedicated agents, if you receive an Admin UAC prompt, see Article 8163 to use admin credentials.

    Step 2: GlobalProtect VPN

    1. It may be necessary to disconnect from GlobalProtect VPN to be able to perform troubleshooting steps on the user's system.

    2. In such a case:

      1. ⚠️ This code should only be entered by the dedicated agent screen-sharing into the system.

      2. Note the reason for disconnecting from GlobalProtect VPN in the Service Order.

      3. In your web browser, open your 1Password extension and enter your password.

      4. On the left side, under "All Items", select GlobalProtect Disable Passcode.

      5. Click the password field to copy the code.

      6. On the user's system, in the "Enter the passcode to disconnect" prompt, paste the code, then click Disconnect.

      7. ⚠️ You MUST reconnect to GlobalProtect VPN before exiting the screen-share.

    3. Proceed to Step 3.

    Step 3: Determine Connection Method

    1. To initiate a screen-share with a company-supported computer, proceed to Step 4.

    2. To initiate a screen-share with a non-company-supported computer, proceed to Step 5.

    Step 4: Connect via Endpoint Central

    1. Open a web browser and navigate to https://endpointcentral.manageengine.com/webclient#.

    2. Sign in using your "mydomain.local" username in the form of pam-wa-(USERNAME).

      1. For instructions on retrieving the "mydomain.local" username and login information from Desktop, see Article 8163.

    3. You will then be taken to the company login where you will need to sign in with the same credentials.

    4. Once signed in, select Tools at the top, then select Remote Control.

    5. Expand the window by clicking the "less than" symbol with three horizontal lines.

    6. Have the caller perform the following:

      1. On your computer, click the Windows Start button then search for and select About your PC.

      2. Please read me the name to the right of "Device name". Note the caller's response in the Service Order.

    7. In the "Devices" tab, to the right of "Total Records" click the magnifying glass symbol.

    8. Search for the computer name noted in Step 4.6.b.

      1. If still not found, proceed to Step 5 to connect via BeyondTrust.

    9. In the "Action" column, click Connect.

    10. On the "endpointcentral.manageengine.com wants to.." prompt, click Allow.

    11. On the right side of the window, in the "View" section, you should see all of the user's monitors listed.

    12. You can select any of the screens to view and control the user's system.

    13. You will also see a small notification at the bottom of the screen confirming that you are connected.

    14. Return to the Originating Article.

    Step 5: Connect via BeyondTrust

    1. Using the Web Rep Console:

      1. In your browser, navigate to https://support.beyondtrustcloud.com/login/downloads/console.

      2. Leave "Authenticate Using" to SAML Credentials and click Login.

      3. If prompted, enter your company credentials.

      4. On the "Consoles & Downloads" page, click Launch Web Rep Console.

      5. Generate a session key by either clicking Generate Session Key under "My Queues", or by clicking Start at the top-left.

      6. Proceed to Step 5.3.

    2. Using the Desktop Representative Console:

      1. Open the BeyondTrust Remote Support application.

      2. On the login screen, leave "Authenticate Using" to SAML Credentials and click Login.

      3. On the browser page that pops up, complete the login using your company credentials.

      4. At the top-left, click Start.

      5. In the "Start Support Session" window, click Generated Session Key.

      6. Proceed below.

    3. The "Support Session Key" window will open.

    4. ⚠️ Do not send the link via email, especially if you do not have the user on the line (Callback scenarios).

    5. Instruct the user: In your browser, navigate to https://support.company.com.

    6. Provide the session key to the user and have them click Submit.

    7. Instruct the user: In the pop-up, enter your name and click Submit.

    8. The user will be listed in queue. Double-click the session.

    9. At the top, click the Web Chat tab, then click Full Customer Client.

    10. Instruct the user: On the prompt, click Accept.

    11. Instruct the user: A file will download. Click the file to run it when the download is complete.

    12. ⚠️ When you have finished with the screen-share, log out of BeyondTrust. Only 10 users can be logged in at a time.

    13. Return to the Originating Article.
